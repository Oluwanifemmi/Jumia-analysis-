# Jumia-analysis-
Jumia is an online marketplace in Africa for electronics ,with one billion annual visits largely dominated by the third party sellers This analysis is focused on phone brand ,review ,rom,ram and battery capacity as it’ll help the customers to choose the best brand of phone. Summary  1.From this analysis price of phone don’t determine the quality of the phone  2.The battery capacity has less effect on price while Ram/Rom has a huge effect on price. 3.Phone price has no significant effect on the number of time reviewed and ratings. 4.Also discount has less effect on the number of time reviewed.   #dataanalytics #msexcel #excel #dashboard #microsoftoffice #visualizations #datamining #r #tableau
